Kaleidoscope

Crafted by Sarina Chen

A kaleidoscope is an optical instrument that creates symmetric patterns with mirrors and colored bits of glass or paper. This is a kaleidoscope maker. Follow the directions to make your own art.

  1. Choose the appearance of object using the configurations below. Click RANDOMIZE if you can't decide.
  2. Click the canvas on the right to place your object.
  3. Click SHAKE to watch your kaleidoscope in action.
